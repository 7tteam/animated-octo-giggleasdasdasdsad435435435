‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I earn coins?
You can earn coins once every 24 hours. The timer resets at midnight UTC, so make sure to claim your daily reward!
What happens if I close the page before the timer ends?
The timer will reset if you close or refresh the page. Make sure to stay on the page for the full 2 minutes to claim your reward.
Are the server upgrades permanent?
Yes! CPU, RAM, and disk upgrades are permanent. However, server renewals extend your server's lifetime for the specified period (usually 1 month).
Can I transfer coins to another user?
Currently, coin transfers between users are not supported. Coins are tied to your account and can only be used for your own server upgrades.
What if I don't have enough coins for an upgrade?
Keep earning daily! You can also check back for special promotions or bonus coin opportunities that we announce periodically.
